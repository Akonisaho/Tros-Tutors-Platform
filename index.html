About Us

The Rose Of Sharon Tutors

TROS Tutors provides affordable tutoring services to students in need. The organization is committed to helping students achieve academic success and reach their full potential. The tutors are highly qualified and experienced, and they work closely with students to provide personalized support and guidance. The Rose of Sharon Tutors is dedicated to making a positive impact in the lives of students and their families, and they are passionate about helping students succeed.

Services

We tutor students from grade R-7

From Monday to Friday, between 14:30 and 18:00.

We offer the following subjects:

  • Social Sciences
  • Natural Sciences
  • EMS
  • English
  • Mathematics

Payments are as follows:

  • Grade R-4: R230
  • Grade 5-7: R250
